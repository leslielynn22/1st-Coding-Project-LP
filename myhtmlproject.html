Leslie's Opinion of Video Games

Game Name Type (PC/XBOX) My Rating (1-10) Notes
Resident Evil Both 7 Suspenseful and fun. Tricky with simple buttons and limited options.
Serious Sam XBOX 8 Funny and over the top. Fun to wander the map and find the secrets.
Gears of War XBOX 10 Hoard mode is the best. Fun to experiment with the weapons and alterations. Good storyline as well.
Halo XBOX 5 Good story line. Hard to manage controls, visually made me dizzy.
